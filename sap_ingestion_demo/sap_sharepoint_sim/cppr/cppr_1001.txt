Document Type: CPPR
Reference: CPPR-1000-Q1
Business Unit: 1000

Summary

This document records the prioritisation outcomes for change requests raised
against the finance module during the reporting period. Requests were scored
against business impact, implementation effort and regulatory deadline
proximity.

Findings

Three requests were escalated for steering committee review. Two were
deferred to the following quarter due to resourcing constraints. One was
approved for immediate implementation following a risk assessment.

Recommendation

Proceed with the approved request under standard change control procedures.
Revisit deferred items at the start of the next quarter.
