Document Type: CPPR
Reference: CPPR-2000-Q1
Business Unit: 2000

Summary

This document records the prioritisation outcomes for change requests raised
against the logistics module during the reporting period. Requests were
scored against business impact, implementation effort and downstream system
dependencies.

Findings

Four requests were reviewed. One was rejected due to insufficient business
case. Three were approved with staged rollout dates across the next two
quarters.

Recommendation

Proceed with staged rollout as scheduled. Reassess the rejected request if
the business case is resubmitted with updated cost estimates.
